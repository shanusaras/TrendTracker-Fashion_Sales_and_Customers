The database consists of four tables: customers, orders, products, and sales.

1. Customers table: This table stores various customer information, including customer_id, customer_name, gender, age, home_address, zip_code, city, state, and country.

2. Orders table: This table contains order-related information, including order_id, customer_id, order_date, and delivery_date.

3. Products table: This table contains product information, including product_id, product_type, product_name, size, color, price, quantity, and description.

4. Sales table: This table contains detailed sales information, including sales_id, order_id, product_id, price_per_unit, quantity, and total_price.
